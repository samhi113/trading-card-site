Who is the WDDA?

The Web Designers and Developers Association is a student organization with the goal of helping student in the Web (and greater tech field) develop into prepared and knowledgeable members of the Luddy and greater developer communities.

What do we do?

The WDDA hosts events such as development nights, speaker events, and how-to sessions. Need help with a class? Want to have some help with a project? We can help you!
